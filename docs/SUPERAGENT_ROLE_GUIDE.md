## â“ FAQ

**Q: Why can't I see Reports?**  
A: Reports are restricted to Admin and Superadmin. Request access from your administrator if needed.

**Q: Why don't I appear in agent dropdowns?**  
A: Superagents are coordinators, not working agents. Only regular agents can be assigned to customers.

**Q: Can I handle customers myself?**  
A: No, your role is to coordinate and assign. Use the Agent role if you need to handle customers directly.

**Q: Can I create new agents?**  
A: No, user management is restricted to Admin and Superadmin.

**Q: What if I need reports access?**  
A: Contact your administrator to upgrade your role to Admin.

**Q: Can I see all customer data?**  
A: Yes, you have full visibility of all customers for coordination purposes.
